Smart glasses: turning obstacles into announcements.

Smart sunglasses for the visually impaired – making navigation safe and independent.

Why It Matters

Over 39 million people worldwide are blind. Our mission is to empower them with independence and confidence.

"Technology should see for those who cannot."

Benefits

  • Enhances mobility and independence for visually impaired users.
  • Real-time obstacle detection to prevent accidents.
  • Lightweight and comfortable design for daily use.
  • Easy to operate with intuitive feedback (vibration or audio).
  • Rechargeable battery for long-lasting performance.

Specifications

  • Ultrasonic sensors for obstacle detection (range: up to 3 meters)
  • Vibration motor and/or audio feedback
  • Rechargeable Li-ion battery (8 hours usage)
  • Lightweight frame (less than 50g)
  • Micro-USB charging port
  • LED status indicator

How to Use

  1. Charge the glasses using the provided USB cable.
  2. Wear the glasses comfortably on your face.
  3. Switch on the device using the power button.
  4. Move forward; the glasses will alert you to obstacles via vibration or sound.
  5. Recharge as needed after use.
